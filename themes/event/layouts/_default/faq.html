FAQ

Are the ceremony and reception at the same venue?

Yes! The ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception will be at Brick and Mortar (address can be found in the Travel tab). The ceremony and cocktail hour will be held in the outdoor courtyard, and the reception will be indoors.

What time should I arrive?

Guests may start arriving at 5:00 PM for the ceremony at 5:30 PM. Please plan to arrive a minimum of 15 minutes early to accommodate for parking and getting seated before the ceremony.

Is there parking?

Yes, our venue has plenty of free parking available on-site.

What is the dress code?

We appreciate you wearing your best cocktail or semi-formal attire.

Are my children invited?

Although we love your little ones, the wedding is an adults only evening. Thank you for making arrangements ahead of time and leaving the kids at home.

Do you have a registry?

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for! For those who wish, we have a registry.
